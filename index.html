The Number Guessing Game!

the mystery number. The mystery number is chosen at the beginning
of every run and you will have four guesses to try and figure out
what number it is. You will recieve hints in the clue section to
guess either higher or lower than your previous guess. SO to get
started go ahead and put your guess in the input and select
